# Demo Video Script — 2 min 30 sec

## Specs

- **Length:** 2 min 30 sec (Shopify recommends 30s-3min; sweet spot is 2-3 min)
- **Resolution:** 1920 × 1080, 30 fps
- **Format:** MP4, H.264, ≤ 100 MB
- **Audio:** Voiceover with quiet background music. Music at -20 to -24 dB so voice sits on top.
- **Where to host:** Upload to YouTube as unlisted, then link from the Shopify listing. (YouTube unlisted means it's not discoverable via search, but works on the listing page.)
- **Captions:** Burn-in captions OR upload an SRT to YouTube. Required for accessibility + many viewers watch muted.

## Tools

- **Screen recording:** Loom, ScreenStudio (Mac), OBS (Win), or QuickTime
- **Edit:** ScreenStudio if you have a Mac (best UX for app demos), DaVinci Resolve free if Windows, or Descript (browser-based)
- **VO recording:** Built-in laptop mic is fine for a v1. Just record in a small, quiet room. Or hire a 30/min voice actor on Fiverr if you don't want your own voice on it.

## Structure (per Shopify's pattern for high-performing app videos)

1. **0:00 – 0:08** — Hook (problem statement)
2. **0:08 – 0:25** — Show the editor in action
3. **0:25 – 0:50** — AI assistant demo
4. **0:50 – 1:15** — SEO scoring + social preview
5. **1:15 – 1:35** — Internal links + Article Health
6. **1:35 – 1:55** — Live preview + autosave
7. **1:55 – 2:20** — Pricing + plan badge
8. **2:20 – 2:30** — CTA + outro

## Full script

### 0:00 — 0:08 · Hook (cold open)

**Visual:** Cut directly to a blank Shopify article editor in the admin (the default Shopify blog editor — NOT BlockPress). Show a writer struggling to format a post. Cursor scrolling, nothing happening.

## Production notes

- **Speed up cursor movement** in post-production. Real cursor movement looks sluggish on video. Apply a 1.3-1.5x time stretch on all "between-click" segments while keeping the click moments at real speed.
- **Zoom in on UI** for the AI Assistant and SEO score moments — these are the showcase features. ScreenStudio does this auto. In other tools, manually crop to 80% on those segments.
- **Don't read every menu label aloud.** The visual carries it. The VO is the why, the screen is the what.
- **Music:** Royalty-free from Artlist or Epidemic Sound. Something upbeat-but-focused (think dev-tool launch video vibes — Stripe, Linear). Avoid corporate-stock.

---

## Common mistakes to avoid

- ❌ Don't start with "Hi, I'm Rodney" — Shopify reviewers have watched 10,000 of those. Cold open with the problem.
- ❌ Don't show the install flow. Reviewers know how OAuth works.
- ❌ Don't show every feature. The 7 above are the heaviest hitters. Anything else dilutes.
- ❌ Don't use the Shopify default theme dawn purple — your real brand colors (BlockPress blue + gold) photograph better on the listing.
